About

Canlis is a fine‑dining restaurant in Seattle’s Queen Anne neighborhood. The focus is hospitality, Pacific Northwest ingredients, and a calm dining room. Expect a thoughtful tasting menu and professional service.

  • Reservations recommended
  • Smart casual to formal dress is common
  • Valet and rideshare friendly
  • Great for celebrations and special nights
